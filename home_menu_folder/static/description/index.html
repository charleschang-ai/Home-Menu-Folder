Home Menu App Folders | iOS-Style App Organizer | Drag & Drop Folders | Auto-Organize by Category | Home Screen Launcher | App Grouping | Dark Mode

Too many apps on your Odoo home screen? Organize them exactly like your phone. Drag one app onto another to create a beautiful folder, hold it over a folder to spring it open, and drop it right where you want. One click groups every app by its official Odoo category — one more click undoes it. Every user keeps their own layout, stored safely on the server.



iOS-Style Folders — Create Them with a Simple Drag

- Drag one app onto the center of another app: a folder is created instantly. - Drag more apps onto the folder to add them — the target highlights so you always know what will happen before you release. - Drag between tiles to reorder, exactly like a phone home screen. - Frosted-glass folder tiles with animated gradient borders and a 2x2 live icon preview.



Spring Loading — Drop Apps Exactly Where You Want

- Hold a dragged app over a folder for one second: the folder zooms open under your cursor. - Keep dragging inside the opened panel and drop the app at the precise position you want. - Release outside the panel to cancel — nothing moves unless you say so.


One-Click Auto-Organize by Official Odoo Categories

- A single click groups every app into folders by its official Odoo module category: Sales, Finance, Human Resources, Inventory, Website, Marketing, Services and more. - Folder names follow your database language automatically. - Changed your mind? One-click Undo restores your previous layout instantly. - A companion "Dissolve all folders" button flattens everything back to a plain grid — also with Undo. - Both maintenance actions are available to administrators in developer mode, keeping the interface clean for everyday users.



The Folder Panel — Rename, Reorder, Unpack

- Click a folder and it zooms open from its own tile, just like iOS. - Rename the folder inline, reorder the apps inside, or launch any app directly. - Drag an app outside the panel to move it back to the home screen — a clear "release to remove" hint shows before you drop. - Folders dissolve automatically when only one app remains. Escape closes the panel first, then the home menu.




Beautiful in Light and Dark — Informative at a Glance

- App count badge on every folder corner, "+N" indicator when a folder holds more than four apps. - Dedicated dark-mode styling out of the box. - Simplified Chinese translation included.




Safe by Design — Per User, No New Tables

- Every user arranges their own home screen; layouts are stored server-side and follow the user across browsers and devices. - Built on Odoo's native home-menu configuration storage: no new database tables, no risk to your data. - Uninstall at any time and the standard Odoo home menu simply comes back.
